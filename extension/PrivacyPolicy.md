# Privacy Policy - Polymarket Baccarat Extension

**Last Updated: 2026**

This extension, "Polymarket Baccarat Board," is designed strictly to visualize public historical data available on the Polymarket platform. We respect your privacy.

## Data Collection
1. **URL Tracking**: We strictly request the `webNavigation` and `host_permissions` specifically limited to `*.polymarket.com`. The extension reads the active market URL to determine which historical data to query and display. It does not track your browsing history on any other websites.
2. **Authentication**: If you choose to upgrade to Premium, we use Supabase to authenticate your Google profile or Email so we can verify your subscription tier. We store your email address solely for account management. 
3. **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell, rent, or distribute any user data. Period.

## Opt-Out
If you do not want the extension to read the active Polymarket URL, simply disable or uninstall the extension. There are no background trackers.

## Contact
If you have any questions, please reach out via the Chrome Web Store Support tab.
